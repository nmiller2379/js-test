Javascript test

This Javascript test consts of five multiple-choice questions. It is timed. You will have 2 minutes to complete the test. When a question is answered correctly, the next question will appear. For each question answered incorrectly you will lose 30 seconds in your remaining time to complete the test.

Once your test is complete — either because all the questions have been answered or the time has elapsed — you will see your score. Be sure to enter your initials and save your score to see how you compare to others who have taken the test.
